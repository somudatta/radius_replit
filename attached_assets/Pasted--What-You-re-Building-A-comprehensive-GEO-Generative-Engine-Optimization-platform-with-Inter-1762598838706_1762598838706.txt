üîç What Each Metric Measures
AIC (40% weight) - Answerability & Intent Coverage

s1: Intent Coverage - Do you answer all user questions?
s2: Directness - How quickly do you get to the answer?
s3: Completeness - Is your content comprehensive?
s4: Consistency - Is info up-to-date and consistent?
s5: Conversational - Is it AI-conversation ready?

CES (35% weight) - Credibility, Evidence & Safety

s1: Evidence Density - Citations, stats, facts
s2: Author Transparency - Who wrote this?
s3: Experience & Originality - First-hand expertise?
s4: Safety & Disclaimers - Appropriate warnings?
s5: Freshness - Is content current?

MTS (25% weight) - Machine-Readability & Technical

s1: Structure & Semantics - HTML quality
s2: Schema & Metadata - Structured data markup
s3: Entity Clarity - Clear definitions
s4: Crawlability - Can AI access content?
s5: Reusability - Easy to extract?
